How well can you cooperate with your friends and create a painting?
In the lobby, set your name by clicking on the name with the black arrow. Once you and 3 other fellow artists are ready, the timer will begin.
You and your friends must draw one of the random images that comes up in the left panel. Each artist is in charge of a different set of colors. How closely can you draw the image that comes up?
