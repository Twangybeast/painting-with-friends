Painting
with Friends

View Gallery
How well can you cooperate with your friends and create a painting? In the lobby, set your name by clicking on the name with the black arrow. Once you and 3 other fellow artists are ready, the timer will begin. You and 3 other friends must draw one of the random images that comes up in the left panel. Each is in charge of a different set of colors. Change your color to one of your individual options in the bottom right corner. How closely can you draw the image that comes up?
